About Me

January 19, 2025

Originally from Barcelona, Spain, I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Intelligent Manufacturing Technology at the National Taiwan University of Science and Technology. My tenure as a Wireless Engineer at Huawei Netherlands, where I was involved in the integration of new 5G cellular bands and mobile network expansion, has provided me with valuable hands-on experience. I am a responsible and analytical team player, with a strong work ethic and a commitment to continuous learning. My international experience, including my time in Spain, Netherlands and Taiwan, has given me a global perspective and the ability to work effectively in diverse and multicultural teams. I am fluent in Spanish, English and Catalan.
